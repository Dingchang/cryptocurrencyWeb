Cryptocurrency Project

Project idea

As cryptocurrency becomes a new trend for investment, its unpredictability has caused a lot of troubles for buyers. Our project will build a website dedicated to monitoring the prices of mainstream cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. Users can choose one or multiple cryptocurrencies and the website will display their price in real-time. Users can also sign up emails so that they would get notified if the price of a cryptocurrency crosses a threshold they set.
