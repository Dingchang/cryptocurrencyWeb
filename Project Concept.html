Cryptocurrency Project

Project idea

As cryptocurrency becomes a new trend for investment, its unpredictability has caused a lot of troubles for buyers. Our project will build a website dedicated to monitoring the prices of mainstream cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum. Users can choose one or multiple cryptocurrencies and the website will display their price in real-time. We will use graphs to help visualize the displayed data and the graphs will update in real-time as well. Users can also sign up for emails so that they can get notified if the price of a cryptocurrency crosses a threshold that they have set.

API/Dataset

We will use Coinbase as our public API to gather cryptocurrency prices (https://developers.coinbase.com/)

Data to be pushed to client

Our server will continuously poll our crypto API and when it detects a change in price in any of the currencies we support, it will broadcast this price change via websockets to all clients who subscribed for updates for that particular currency.
